Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Fourth Dimension Web Analyzer uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website or use our platform. It outlines what these technologies are, why we use them, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow a site to recognize your device, remember your preferences, and help improve your user experience. Cookies may be set by us (first-party cookies) or by third-party services we integrate with (third-party cookies).

Why We Use Cookies

Fourth Dimension Web Analyzer uses cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Access

To allow you to log in and securely use the dashboard and account areas.

Session Management

To manage your current session and track basic user interactions like scan history and navigation.

Performance & Analytics

To understand how the platform is used and improve product performance.

User Preferences

To remember UI configurations like theme settings for a smoother experience.

Privacy First

We do not use cookies for advertising or behavioral tracking. Our cookie usage is focused on functionality and product improvement.

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Example
Strictly Necessary Required for core platform functions such as login and secure browsing session_id, csrf_token
Functional Help remember your preferences across sessions user_theme, locale_pref
Analytics Help us understand how users interact with features and flows _ga, _gid (Google Analytics)

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services to help us monitor system performance or gather anonymized usage insights. These third-party services may set their own cookies in your browser. For example:

  • Google Analytics may store a cookie to differentiate new and returning users
  • Cloudflare or CDN providers may use cookies to optimize security or traffic routing

Fourth Dimension Web Analyzer does not allow third-party advertisers or social media trackers to place cookies on our platform.

How You Can Control Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Most modern browsers provide settings to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Clear existing cookies
  • Set exceptions for trusted websites

Important Note

Blocking essential cookies may affect your ability to use some features of the Fourth Dimension Web Analyzer platform, such as logging in or saving settings.
